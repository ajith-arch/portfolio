A bit about me

Hi, I’m Ajith Alphonse, currently pursuing my Master’s in Information Systems at the University of Utah, where I focus on design and development. Before that, I completed my Bachelor’s in Architecture and Digital Design in India, along with a minor in User Experience Design, which basically means I’ve always been curious about how things look and how they work.

My design journey started at 15 when I began building websites, creating templates, and experimenting with graphic design simply because I loved it. What started as curiosity slowly turned into a career. I also enjoy drawing human face portraits, a hobby that keeps my eye sharp and strengthens my sense of detail and visual storytelling.

Over the past five years, I’ve worked in UX and product design across healthcare, fintech, education, and e-commerce. Currently, I’m working as a UI/UX Designer at the University of Utah Campus Store, where I continue to learn, build, and refine my craft while growing as a designer every day.
