The merchandising team, admins, and developers all interact with this system daily to maintain revenue-generating product listings for the university's e-commerce platform.

Reflection

What I learned

"This project shifted my focus from visual redesign to workflow optimization within enterprise systems. I learned that reducing cognitive friction can have greater business impact than visual refresh alone."
01

Enterprise Constraints

Working within existing systems requires creative solutions that respect technical boundaries.

02

Workflow Over Visuals

The biggest impact came from restructuring the flow, not redesigning the interface.

03

AI as Assistant

AI should augment human decision-making, not replace it. User control is essential.

04

Business Awareness

Understanding revenue impact and stakeholder priorities shapes design decisions.
